who we are

ETE, Engineering Co. for Transport & Equipment, is one of the providers of transportation, equipment and logistical services in Egypt. ETE is one of the sister companies under mh group, providing transportation services and high-end rental equipment. We are proud to present a unique business model to the market.

With over 60+ years of combined experience in varying fields, our experts know the ins and outs of the field, continuously striving to enhance the customer experience from start to finish.

ETE’s Mission is to become a top logistical solutions company in the region by delivering high-end logistical services and equipment to all clients.

ETE’s Vision to become the company of choice and industry leader for logistic solutions and equipment in Egypt.

ETE's Value we ensure that all our services, whether renting equipment or providing transportation, exceed all safety standards.

What we do

At ETE, we provide the following solutions with onestop solutions for all your logistical needs, Our repertoire of services are consistently advancing and tailoring to the current requirements of the markets we serve.

Construction Fleets

ETE knows how important it is to have an efficient resources ready to lift a site into reality.

All resources such as cranes, loaders and heavy duty tracks needed for the projects are provided and maintained by ETE.

Shipping Solutions

Our state-of-the-art, smart solution equipped trucks can transport freight shipments in and out of the country in a timely and cost effective manner. The types of trucks that can be rented are:

-Trucks (T-series, Jumbo, Double Cabin, Suzuki van, Mercedes Acros with or without refrigerator)

Through our smart solutions and GPS tracking, our clientele will be able to track their shipments through their personal devices personally on our ETE application.

ETE is confident about the prospects of expanding into aerial, air and maritime shipments in the near future.

RENTAL OPPORTUNITIES (AUTOMOTIVE)

With ETE, you may rent out all kinds of vehicles to suit your business needs with or without drivers.

Whether a sedan, van, or SUV, you can rent out cars at very competitive prices and top security measures for whatever duration you desire, be it daily or monthly. All trips can be monitored through a GPS tracking system to know where the fleet has progressed to.

Our professionally attired drivers go through strenuous, on-going trainings to make any journey they take safe, precise and successful. We make sure to provide worldwide standards of safety through our rental services.

We aim to be a one-stop tailored solution to ensure on-time arrivals, enjoyable end-to-end customer experiences.
